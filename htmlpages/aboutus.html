About us

Toyotoys.pk sells award-winning Toyotoys and learning material for children that help them to learn and grow well. Operated by a team of educationists trained at the top universities of Pakistan, all the toys and other learning material listed on the website have been researched and hand-selected for their demonstration in support of healthy child development.

We stock a wide variety of Toyotoys including toys that develop motor skills, improve language and mathematical skills, wooden toys, flashcards, pretend-play toys, and early learning resources. These child developmental toys are all designed to stimulate activity and creativity among infants, toddlers, and younger children. We also have a fantastic choice of STEM kits, puzzles, and board games to challenge older children including teenagers.

Who are we?

Toyotoys Pakistan was founded in 2020 by Atiqa Aly. She has completed her MPhil in Education from Aga Khan University – Institute of Educational Development. She also has a diploma in Early Childhood Education with several years of teaching and professional development experience. She is assisted by an excellent team helping her manage this business.
